Work

Welcome to Level Head, where tranquility meets precision in music management. Our philosophy is simple: we believe in the power of a calm, focused approach to elevate your music career. We prioritize the needs of the artist above all else, recognizing that each journey is unique and deserving of personalized attention.

With a commitment to nurturing creativity and fostering growth,our team is dedicated to guiding you through every step of the process with care and expertise. From strategic planning to day-to-day management, we are here to ensure that your artistic vision is realized to its fullest potential. Join us at Level Head, where serenity and success go hand in hand.

About

Our founders, Franklin and Naddem, started managing a music venue in college and found a love for connecting with musicians and culitvating community. Collaboraiting on events with artists from Lil B to Beach Fossils, they saw firsthand the power of music to change the world and wanted to be a part of bringing that energy to the world. This led to the creation of Level Head - a music management company focused on supporting and uplifting artists early in their careers to grow to their own fullest potential.

Franklin holds a B.S.E in Operations Research from Princeton University, while Nadeem brings to the table a Masters in Environmental Management and International & Development Economics from Yale University, along with his undergraduate education at Princeton.
